Rainbow Quality Reference Image

This file should contain an image showing examples of different rainbow quality levels:

1/3 Stars - Faint Rainbow:
- Pale colors, limited visibility
- Often appears against light backgrounds
- Muted spectrum with weak contrast

2/3 Stars - Moderate Rainbow:
- Clear color bands visible
- Good contrast against dark sky
- Distinct arc formation

3/3 Stars - Spectacular Rainbow:
- Vivid, saturated colors
- Full spectrum clearly visible
- Possible double rainbow
- Perfect for photography

The image should demonstrate how atmospheric conditions affect rainbow visibility and color intensity, helping users understand the quality rating system.
